[Dr. Daoyuan Wu](https://daoyuan14.github.io/) is currently an Assistant Professor at the School of Data Science, Lingnan University (LU), where he leads the AIS2Lab (Artificial Intelligence and Systems Security Lab).

Prior to joining LU, Dr. Wu was a Research Assistant Professor (RAP) at HKUST CSE for one year, a Senior Research Fellow at Nanyang Technological University for one year, and a Senior Researcher at Huawei HKRC for six months. Before that, he spent three years as a fully independent RAP in the Department of Information Engineering (IE) at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). He also served as an Adjunct Assistant Professor at CUHK IE for one year, from 2022 to 2023.

Dr. Wu has published 29 CCF-A conf papers and 7 CCF-A journal articles, including 13 big-four security conf papers, 11 top-tier SE conf papers, and a Distinguished Paper Award at NDSS 2025. He has served as a regular TPC member at top-tier Security, SE, and AI conferences, as well as a Youth Editorial Board Member at the Cybersecurity journal. He has been the PI for the Stellar SDF Academic Research Award, the CCF-Huawei Populus Grove Fund for Systems, and several UGC-funded courseware development grants, as well as a Co-PI for an RGC CRF and a Co-I for an RGC GRF project.
